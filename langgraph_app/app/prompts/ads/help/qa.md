Question:
How will Headlines and Details pair together?

Answer:
Google automatically combines pairs of Headlines and Details to find the strongest performing ads. You don't have direct control over the pairing, but you can influence the results by providing high-quality, varied options for both headlines and details.

Question:
What are descriptions?

Answer:
In Google Ads terms, descriptions are the longer text that appears below your headline in a search ad. They provide additional details about your business, product, or service and give users more context about what you're offering.
Google's requirements:

Minimum: 2 descriptions required
Maximum: 4 descriptions allowed
Character limit: Typically 90 characters per description (though this specific limit isn't mentioned in your document)

Purpose:
Descriptions expand on your headline by explaining what your business does, the problem you solve, or encouraging users to take action. While headlines grab attention, descriptions provide the supporting information that helps convince someone to click on your ad.
