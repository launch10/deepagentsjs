# Brainstorming: Social Proof

Credibility = testimonials OR founder story OR track record OR credentials OR proof

## Hormozi Proof Hierarchy

**High-impact**: Quantified results, case studies, volume, authority
**Medium-impact**: Generic testimonials, credentials, years
**Low-impact**: Self-reported, vague numbers

## For Early-Stage (No Testimonials)

Founder credibility, personal transformation, research/process, community size = valid proof

## Key Probes

- "No testimonials" → Ask about background, personal results, beta users, research
- Generic praise → Push for numbers, timeline, transformation
- Too much proof → Select most compelling 2-3

## Edge Cases

- Privacy/NDA → Industry descriptors OK
- Pre-launch → Founder story CRITICAL
