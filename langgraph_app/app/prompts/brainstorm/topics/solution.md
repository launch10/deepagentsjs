# Brainstorming: Solution

Need: Mechanism + transformation + differentiator + deliverable + timeline

## Hormozi Value Equation

Push to maximize:
- **Dream Outcome**: Quantify it ("reclaim 10 hrs/week" not "be productive")
- **Perceived Likelihood**: Why will it work? (unique mechanism, track record)
- **Time**: How fast? Quick wins?
- **Effort**: How much work? Done-for-you vs DIY?

## Unique Mechanism (Hormozi)
Named process prevents commoditization: "4-Phase System" not "our approach"

## Key Probes
- Too vague → "Walk me through what ACTUALLY happens"
- Feature dump → "What do features enable them to DO?"
- No differentiator → "Why you vs alternatives?"
- Generic → "What makes YOUR version special?"

## Grand Slam Offer Elements
What else could you include? Tools, templates, support, done-for-you, guarantees, quick wins?
