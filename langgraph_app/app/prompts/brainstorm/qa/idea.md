# QA: Business Idea

Looking for:
1. What they do (not just industry)
2. Who it's for (even vague is OK here)
3. What makes it different (some hook/angle)
4. Why it matters (problem solved)

**"I'm still figuring it out"** → success: true if directional enough
**Multiple ideas** → success: false, pick one
**Just industry name** ("I'm in fitness") → fail, need more
**Buzzword soup** → fail, need plain language
