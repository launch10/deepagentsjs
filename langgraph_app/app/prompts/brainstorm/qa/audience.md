# QA: Audience

Need: Demographics + psychographics + situation + pain/aspiration

**Godin's Smallest Viable Audience**: Who would be devastated if this didn't exist?

**Weak patterns to reject**:
- "Anyone who wants X" 
- Pure demographics: "25-45 year olds"
- "Small business owners" (too generic)
- Missing the "why now"

**Multiple audiences** → fail, pick ONE
**Niche anxiety** ("excluding too many!") → this is GOOD, reassure them
