# QA: Solution

Need: Mechanism + transformation + differentiator + deliverable

**Hormozi Value Equation** - Score mentally:
- Dream Outcome: Specific & quantified? (not "more productive")
- Perceived Likelihood: WHY it works? (method, track record)
- Time Delay: Timeline mentioned?
- Effort & Sacrifice: Level of work clear?

**If 3+ score <7 → fail**

**Unique Mechanism**: Named process = strong ("4-Phase System" not "our approach")

**Reject**:
- Feature dumps without benefits
- "Better/faster/cheaper" (weak differentiation)
- Vague "I help X do Y"
- Unclear done-for-you vs DIY
