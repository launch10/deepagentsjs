# QA: Social Proof

**Social proof is broader than testimonials**: founder story, background, personal results, research, community size all count.

**For early-stage (no testimonials)**: Founder credibility, personal transformation, process credibility, relatability = valid proof

**High-impact proof** (Hormozi):
- Quantified results: "Average client adds $50K"
- Specific case studies with numbers
- Volume: "500+ businesses"
- Authority: "Featured in Forbes"

**Reject**:
- "I don't have testimonials" without offering alternatives
- Generic praise without specifics ("clients love me")
- Vague credentials ("10 years experience" without context)

**Privacy/NDA** → industry descriptors OK ("Fortune 500 SaaS")
