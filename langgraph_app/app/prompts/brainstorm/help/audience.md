TOPIC: Target Audience

The user needs help describing who their ideal customer is and what problems they face.

SPECIFIC GUIDANCE FOR THIS TOPIC:

- Break this into 3-4 clear sub-questions
- Sub-questions should build logically: WHO → PROBLEMS → GOALS → (optionally) WHY EXISTING SOLUTIONS FAIL
- Each sub-question should have a bold header and bracketed guidance
- Help them get specific about demographics AND psychographics
- Emphasize connecting problems to goals (they're two sides of same coin)

SUB-QUESTIONS TO INCLUDE:

1. **Who are they?** - Demographics, role, life context
2. **What problems do they face?** - Pain points, frustrations, obstacles
3. **What are they trying to achieve?** - Goals, desired outcomes, aspirations
4. (Optional) **Why don't current solutions work?** - Gap in market

EXAMPLE QUALITY REQUIREMENTS:

- Should paint a vivid picture of a real person
- Must show clear connection between their problems and goals
- Problems should be specific, not generic ("overwhelmed by too much to do")
- Should help them see the emotional dimension, not just functional

BAD EXAMPLE: "Busy professionals who want to save time"
GOOD EXAMPLE: "Marketing managers at 10-50 person SaaS companies who are stretched thin managing multiple channels, feel guilty about inconsistent posting, and want to build thought leadership but can't justify hiring a full-time content person."
