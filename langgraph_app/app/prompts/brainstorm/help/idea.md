TOPIC: Core Business Idea

The user needs help articulating what their business does and what makes it different.

SPECIFIC GUIDANCE FOR THIS TOPIC:

- Provide a single-paragraph template (not multiple sections)
- Template should include: [what you do] + [for whom] + [key benefit] + [unique differentiator]
- Keep it high-level - they shouldn't be listing features
- The template should naturally flow as one cohesive statement
- Help them focus on WHAT makes them different, not just that they ARE different

TEMPLATE COMPONENTS TO INCLUDE:

- Business/product name or description
- Target customer type (specific)
- Main outcome or benefit the customer gets
- How it works or what you provide (brief)
- Unique differentiator or competitive advantage

EXAMPLE QUALITY REQUIREMENTS:

- Should be a complete business concept someone could visualize
- Must show specificity (not vague terms like "innovative solution")
- Should feel authentic, not like marketing copy

BAD EXAMPLE: "We help people be more productive with our innovative app"
GOOD EXAMPLE: "TimeBlocks helps remote workers maintain focus by batching their tasks into 90-minute deep work sessions, tracking energy levels throughout the day to optimize when they tackle different types of work."
